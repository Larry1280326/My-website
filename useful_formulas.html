Useful Formulas

Calculus
Quadratic Formula
\(ax^2 + bx +c =0\)
\(x=\frac{-b\pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)
Compound Angle Formulas
\(sin{(x+y)}=sin{x}\hspace{5px} cos{y}+sin{y}\hspace{5px} cos{x}\)
\(sin{(x-y)}=sin{x}\hspace{5px} cos{y}-sin{y}\hspace{5px} cos{x}\)
\(cos{(x+y)}=cos{x}\hspace{5px} cos{y}-sin{y}\hspace{5px} sin{x}\)
\(cos{(x-y)}=cos{x}\hspace{5px} cos{y}+sin{y}\hspace{5px} sin{x}\)
\(tan{(x+y)}=\frac{tan{x}+tan{y}}{1-tan{x}\hspace{5px} tan{y}} \)
\(tan{(x-y)}=\frac{tan{x}-tan{y}}{1+tan{x}\hspace{5px} tan{y}} \)
Products-to-sums Formulas
\(sin{x}\hspace{5px} sin{y}= \frac{1}{2}\hspace{5px}(cos{(x-y)}-cos{(x+y)})\)
\(cos{x}\hspace{5px} cos{y}= \frac{1}{2}\hspace{5px}(cos{(x+y)}+cos{(x-y)})\)
\(sin{x}\hspace{5px} cos{y}= \frac{1}{2}\hspace{5px}(sin{(x+y)}+cos{(x-y)})\)
Sums-to-products Formulas
\(sin{x}+sin{y}=2\hspace{5px}sin{(\frac{x+y}{2})}\hspace{5px} cos{(\frac{x-y}{2})} \)
\(sin{x}-sin{y}=2\hspace{5px}sin{(\frac{x-y}{2})}\hspace{5px} cos{(\frac{x+y}{2})} \)
\(cos{x}+cos{y}=-2\hspace{5px}sin{(\frac{x+y}{2})}\hspace{5px} sin{(\frac{x-y}{2})} \)
\(cos{x}+cos{y}=2\hspace{5px}cos{(\frac{x+y}{2})}\hspace{5px} cos{(\frac{x-y}{2})} \)
Reduction Formulas
\(\int sin^n{x} \hspace{5px}dx=-\frac{1}{n}\hspace{5px} sin^{n-1}{x}\hspace{5px} cos{x}+\frac{n-1}{n} \hspace{5px} \int sin^{n-2}{x} \hspace{5px} dx \)
\(\int cos^n{x} \hspace{5px}dx=\frac{1}{n}\hspace{5px} cos^{n-1}{x}\hspace{5px} sin{x}+\frac{n-1}{n} \hspace{5px} \int cos^{n-2}{x} \hspace{5px} dx \)
\(\int sec^n{x} \hspace{5px}dx=\frac{1}{n-1}\hspace{5px} sec^{n-2}{x} \hspace{5px} tan{x} +\frac{n-2}{n-1}\hspace{5px} \int sec^{n-2}{x} \hspace{5px} dx \)
\(\int tan^n{x} \hspace{5px}dx=\frac{1}{n-1} \hspace{5px} tan^{n-1}{x}-\int tan^{n-2}{x} \hspace{5px} dx \)
Physics
Lorentz Factor
\(\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}\)
Time Dilation
\(t=\gamma \hspace{5px} t_{0}=\frac{t_{0}}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}\)
Length Contraction
\(l=\frac{l_{0}}{\gamma}=l_{0} \hspace{5px} \sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}} \)
Relative Velocity
\(u^{'} = \frac{u+v}{1+\frac{uv}{c^2}} \)
Relativistic Energy
\(E=\gamma \hspace{5px} m \hspace{5px} c^2 = \frac{m c^2}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \)
Relativistic Momentum
\(p=\gamma \hspace{5px} m \hspace{5px} v = \frac{m v}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \)
Energy-momentum
\(E^2 = p^2 c^2 + m^2 c^4\)
Mass-energy
\(E = m c^2\)
Relativistic Kinetic Energy
\(K = (\gamma -1)\hspace{5px} mc^2 = (\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}} -1)\hspace{5px} mc^2\)
Relativistic Doppler Effect
\(\frac{\lambda}{\lambda_{0}} =\frac{f_{0}}{f}= \sqrt(\frac{1+\frac{v}{c}}{1-\frac{v}{c}}) \)
Photon Energy
\(E=hf = pc\)
Photon Momentum
\(p=\frac{h}{\lambda}=\frac{E}{c} \)
